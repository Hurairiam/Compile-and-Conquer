# Compile and Conquer

A life-simulation RPG built with Python and Pygame where every hour matters.

## About the Game

Compile and Conquer follows the journey of a fresh HSC graduate who dreams of becoming a successful Computer Science engineer. After securing admission to a fictional university, players must navigate four years of academic challenges, skill development, internships, and career decisions.

The goal is simple: graduate on time, build valuable skills, secure a high-paying engineering job, and achieve the future you've always envisioned.

Time is your most valuable resource. Every decision costs or saves hours, and how you spend those hours determines your grades, skills, career opportunities, and ending.

## Core Features

- 4-year Computer Science degree progression
- Semester and week-based time management system
- Course-based academic progression
- Interactive quizzes and exams
- EXP and leveling system
- Side quests and extracurricular activities
- Skill progression across multiple categories
- Internship and career pathways
- Multiple endings based on academic and career performance
- Save and load system using JSON
- Built with Python and Pygame

## Gameplay Loop

1. Manage your semester hours.
2. Attend classes and complete coursework.
3. Pass exams to earn credits and EXP.
4. Complete side quests to develop practical skills.
5. Apply for internships and gain experience.
6. Graduate and enter the job market.
7. Determine your future through your career achievements.

## Technology Stack

- Python
- Pygame
- JSON (Save System)
